Pro bono mental health access

Help people discover independent psychologists who offer accessible care.

Probono Mental Health Finder is designed for the Ghanaian context and focuses on independent psychologists, counsellors, and community professionals who may choose to offer pro bono or reduced-fee sessions, while also surfacing trusted Ghana mental health contacts and emergency lines.
